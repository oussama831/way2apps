BMI Checker

Introduction

The BMI Checker helps you calculate your Body Mass Index quickly. By entering your weight and height, you can understand if you are underweight, normal, overweight, or obese.

How it Works

Simply input your weight in kilograms and your height in centimeters. The app will calculate your BMI using the standard formula and show your weight category.

Tips

  • Measure your weight and height accurately for correct results.
  • Consult a healthcare professional for advice about your BMI.
  • Use this tool for guidance, not as a medical diagnosis.

Example

If your weight is 70kg and height is 170cm, your BMI will be around 24.2, which is considered a normal weight.
