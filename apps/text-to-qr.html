Convert Text to QR

Introduction

This application allows you to quickly convert any text into a QR code. You can type or paste text, generate the QR, and download it for use in websites, documents, or sharing.

How it Works

Simply enter the text you want to convert, click the "Generate QR" button, and the app will create a QR code instantly. You can then copy it or download it as an image.

Tips

  • Make sure your text is accurate before generating the QR code.
  • You can use it for URLs, messages, or any type of text.
  • Keep the QR code safe and share it easily with others.

Example

Enter "Hello Way2Apps!" → Click "Generate QR" → QR code appears → Download or share.
