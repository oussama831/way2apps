Baby Cry Analyzer

Introduction

The Baby Cry Analyzer helps parents understand the possible needs or emotions of their baby by analyzing the sound of their cry. It provides insights into whether the baby might be hungry, tired, uncomfortable, or in pain.

How it Works

Simply record the baby's cry using the app. The tool analyzes the pitch, duration, and intensity of the sound and gives a general interpretation to help parents respond quickly.

Tips

  • Ensure the recording is clear without background noise.
  • Use this tool as a guide, not a medical diagnosis.
  • Always check on the baby physically if unsure about their needs.

Example

If the baby cries with a high-pitched, intense sound repeatedly, the app may suggest the baby is in discomfort or needs attention. Conversely, soft, intermittent cries may indicate tiredness or minor discomfort.
