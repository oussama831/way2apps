Child Growth

Introduction

The Child Growth app helps parents track their child's growth over time. By recording height, weight, and other developmental milestones, parents can ensure their child is growing healthily and identify potential concerns early.

How it Works

Enter your child's measurements regularly into the app. The tool charts growth trends, compares them to standard growth charts, and provides a visual timeline of development.

Tips

  • Record measurements consistently, preferably at the same time of day.
  • Use accurate measuring tools for reliable tracking.
  • Consult a pediatrician if any growth patterns seem unusual.
