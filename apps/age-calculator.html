Age Calculator

Introduction

The Age Calculator app helps you quickly calculate your age based on your birth date. It's simple, fast, and user-friendly.

How it Works

Enter your date of birth, and the calculator will automatically compute your age in years, months, and days. You can also see how many days you've lived!

Tips

Make sure to enter the correct birth date for accurate results. This tool is perfect for planning birthdays, events, or just for fun.

Example

If you were born on January 1, 2000, the calculator will show your exact age as of today, including years, months, and days.
