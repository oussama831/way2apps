Convert Image to Text

Introduction

This app allows you to quickly convert printed or photographed documents into editable text. Simply upload or take a photo, and the app transforms it into text you can copy or download instantly.

How it Works

Using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology, the app reads the text from your image and converts it into editable digital text in seconds.

Tips

For best results, make sure the document is clear and well-lit. Avoid blurry images or poor lighting to improve text recognition accuracy.

Example

You can photograph a printed page, a note, or a screenshot. The app will convert it into editable text that you can copy or save instantly.
